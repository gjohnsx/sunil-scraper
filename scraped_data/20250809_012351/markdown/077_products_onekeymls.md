## First, fast, and hyperlocal.

#### See it first on OneKeyMLS.com

OneKeyMLS.com has the freshest listings in New York. We push our listings to our site every five minutes, straight out of our MLS. With no middleman, you see the newest listings on OneKeyMLS.com before everyone else, even the big third-party portals.

#### Alerts your way

Sign up for free alerts to keep track of the properties you love. Create a free account and start favoriting individual listings, or create and save a search. Set your preferences to instant, daily, or weekly updates via text or email. Change your preferences anytime.

#### Robust neighborhood search

OneKeyMLS.com focuses on our communities and neighborhoods, so you can search by the names of schools and neighborhoods. Individual listings include expanded demographic and school information.

#### Represented by REALTORS®

Every listing on OneKeyMLS.com is represented by a REALTOR® who is a member of the Long Island Board of REALTORS® or the Hudson Gateway Association of REALTORS®. When you work with a REALTOR®, you can trust that you are working with a professional bound by an enforceable Code of Ethics. When you make an inquiry on a listing on OneKeyMLS.com that question goes directly to the office that represents the listing, no one else. So you'll get prompt service and accurate information from your inquiry.
