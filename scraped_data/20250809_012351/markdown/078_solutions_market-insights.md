# Market Insights

### The best of both worlds: Actionable Hot Sheets and Market Watch lists for you, with expert reports for your clients

[Explore Solution Support](https://support.onekeymls.com/hc/en-us/categories/26660094939028-Market-Insights)

### Let Matrix™ surface all the data you need to run your business, then share it with your clients with MarketStats by ShowingTime.

#### Let Matrix™ watch the market

Get the real-time, high-level overview of the specific areas of interest you care about with Matrix's Market Watch reports. It's a dashboard that monitors all the relevant market changes in one place, including new listings, price reductions, sales, and status changes. Data is updated in real-time, so you can stay on top of market trends as they happen. You can customize the geographic area, property types, and price ranges, so you always have the high-level data you need about the markets you serve at the ready.

#### Take action with Hot Sheets in Matrix™

Create detailed, specific Hot Sheets in Matrix™ on market activity based on criteria you define. Pull them up any time so you can see new listings, price changes, or sold properties within a certain timeframe (e.g., the last 24 hours, or the past week). Create  Hot Sheets as you need them to track different areas or types of properties, and each sheet can be tailored with different search criteria. Check your Hot Sheets daily or weekly to get detailed updates on areas you’re closely tracking.

#### Keep your clients up-to-date with MarketStats

Your clients want to know what affects them. Share your knowledge with MarketStats by ShowingTime™ for the MLS. Create beautiful in-depth reports with FastStats, and delve into the state of their neighborhood with important but easy-to-read graphics about housing inventory, pricing trends, days on market, and other key facts you know will affect their home and lives. Or, use InfoSparks to share a quick graphic about the market that is just as impactful, but easy-to-create that positions you as the neighborhood expert. No matter which tool you choose, the same data you see in your Hot Sheets and Market Watch reports in Matrix™ is used to generate your client-friendly graphics and reports in MarketStats, so you can rest assured that you are providing accurate data to your clients (in addition to gorgeous on-demand graphics).
