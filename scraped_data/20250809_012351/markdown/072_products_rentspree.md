## Easy tenant screening with no monthly fees for agents and property owners.

#### Reusable online rental applications

Use Rentspree's secure online application forms to easily send or collect important information from prospective renters. Get the right information the first time, with no back-and-forth. Everything is organized in your account--part of your OneKey® MLS subscription-- with all the details you need. Share an application link via text or email or in your OneKey® MLS listing. Renters love Rentspree because their applications are reusable for 30 days — they pay $20 to apply many times, saving them the expense of multiple application fees.

#### Comprehensive, accurate, and secure screening

Approve the most qualified tenants when you utilize Rentspree's Tenant Screening Report.  Once your applicants have completed their secure online application, an easy-to-read TransUnion Credit Report is prepared for you that provides concise and insightful information on their credit score, payment history, credit inquiries, tradelines, collections and more. It is a soft inquiry and will not damage the applicant's credit score. The report also provides access to criminal records, the most wanted list, and the national sex offender registry. It searches more than 200 million records from national and state databases, ensuring comprehensive screening. Finally, it includes a check for previous evictions, court records, tenant judgements, writs and eviction warrants.

#### Easy, fast, and professional

Assess interested renters from your dashboard. Make informed decisions on an apples-to-apples basis, based on Rentspree's online application, background check,  and Tenant Screening Report. See a completed sample application[here](https://support.rentspree.com/en/a-completed-rentspree-application). Review all of your applicants and let them know about their acceptance. Or, let them down professionally with Rentspree's built-in Accept/Deny tool. Finish up and sign lease agreements and other documents within Rentspree's e-signature platform.
