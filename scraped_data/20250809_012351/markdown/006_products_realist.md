### Streamline your workflow and build your business with professional-grade property intelligence from Realist®, available to Matrix™ users.

[Realist® Support](https://support.onekeymls.com/hc/en-us/articles/29504411971348-Realist-Resources)

## Analyze market trends, visualize spatial data, and help your clients make better decisions.

#### Be the expert

Realist's market dashboard, accessible from within Matrix™, makes it easy to take the market's temperature and stay on top of key indicators. Take in quick stats, including active listings, pending sales, closed sales, average days on market, months of inventory and sales to list price in any county or borough in OneKey® MLS's service area. Speak with confidence about your service area and be the expert clients listen to.

#### Access comprehensive public record data, right within Matrix™

Realist's seamless integration with Matrix™ allows you to access both MLS listings and public records data in one place, so you can streamline your workflow. Instead of hopping from one app to another or conducting manual research, you can focus on Realist's advanced mapping capabilities, including parcel boundary views, flood zones, and other geographic details. Then present it in gorgeous visuals that make it easy for your clients to understand complex information.

#### Build better CMAs with deep insights

Win buyer and seller presentations when you base your comparative market analysis on Realist's data. Use Realist's incredibly comprehensive datasets to lookup property records, including past sales, ownership history, tax assessments, and more to paint a 360 degree picture of the subject property, including Realist's market trends and forecasting tools to adjust for neighborhood sales trends and immediate pricing history to reflect current market realities. You will be the best neighborhood expert in the business.

#### Generate business with advanced search capabilities

Take search to the next level with Realist's advanced filtering options that go beyond simple property searches. Surface opportunities that align with your business objectives. Filter by specific property criteria to reveal opportunities including foreclosures, bank-owned (REO), short sales, and even absentee ownership others may overlook. Realist® includes map filtering, which help agents target specific segments of the market, including locating owners with significant equity.
