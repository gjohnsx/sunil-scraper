# Broker Benefits

##### From managing listings, agents, and compliance to growing and measuring agent performance, brokers need an effective business partner. OneKey® MLS delivers integrated solutions that create continuity, ease, and simplicity

## Enhance your operations with benefits for your agents and your brokerage.

#### Built to serve you

OneKey® MLS is the largest MLS in New York state, with 4,300 real estate offices across Long Island, Manhattan, and the Lower Hudson Valley. Our Board of Managers is composed of working brokers from independently owned and operated real estate brokerage firms as well as large franchises. This unique structure provides our management team with the strategic insight to select and integrate the right tools to serve you in today’s dynamic market.

#### Integrated and intelligent

OneKey® MLS utilizes the Matrix™ platform, the premier MLS platform in the industry and is natively integrated with Realist® for public records and tax data. With more than a million active and historical listings, we offer our customers a wealth of RESO-standard data that enables them to create better listings at the outset, opens up a wealth of search options, and creates a better workflow for your office through data delivery.

#### Office-level preferences

As the broker, you’re always in control. You determine the permissions, configuration, branding, and data delivery for your back office systems. Ask for a back office data feed to seamlessly push comprehensive data into your intranet, so that your office moves at the speed of business.

#### Validate performance

Utilize OneKey® MLS’s comprehensive market reporting to identify and monitor sales trends, local market conditions, inventory levels, and pricing strategies to help your agents compete. Run productivity reports straight out of Matrix™ to identify top producers and competitors, or those who may need additional support or training. Or, use ListTrac to help your agents show their clients how their listings are performing.

#### Ensure compliance

Avoid simple but costly mistakes and leverage OneKey® MLS’s comprehensive rules and regulations to keep your agents in compliance with New York State real estate law. From approved forms to compliance checklists, OneKey® MLS’s workflow and tools are designed to keep every transaction on track. Take advantage of our online learning and support knowledgebase, on-demand training, and participate in OneKey® MLS leadership.
