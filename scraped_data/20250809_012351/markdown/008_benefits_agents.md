# Agent Benefits

##### With premier technology, white glove service, and a commitment to creating opportunities for every real estate professional, OneKey® MLS is the best multiple listing service in New York

## Get the listing data, service, and insight you need to run your real estate business.

#### Accurate listing data

OneKey® MLS is the largest MLS in New York state, with more than one million active, pending, and historical listings in our database. Our strict business rules ensure our data is timely and reliable, and ensure that your fellow real estate professionals are as responsible as you are. From pulling comps to running market stats or entering a new listing, our processes are straightforward and easy to integrate into your business.

#### Engage and grow

OneKey® MLS doesn't just aggregate and manage listing data. Our job is to create opportunities for you. We own and operate[OneKeyMLS.com](https://onekeymls.com/), the premier property search portal in the New York metropolitan area. We follow a your-listing-your-lead approach, so that you get every lead for every one of your listings. We drive traffic to OneKeyMLS.com using paid search and strategic SEO, delivering thousands of leads each month to our real estate professionals.

#### Integrated tools, easy workflow

All of the tools and services included with your OneKey® MLS subscription are integrated with our listing data, delivering cross-functional usability right off the starting line. For example, get a lead from OneKeyMLS.com, add the contact and create an auto email in Matrix™, collaborate with your new client in OneHome™, schedule showings in ShowingTime™ for the MLS, pull contracts from TransactionDesk®, and show the client property lines in the field with MLS-Touch® -- all through your subscription to OneKey® MLS.

#### Maximize exposure and reach

OneKey® MLS’s 43,000 real estate professionals provide enormous exposure for your listings because they work in tandem to market, buy, sell, and rent listings throughout the metropolitan New York area. You can also use Matrix™ to reverse prospect for buyers for your listings, an easy and fast way to match consumers who are actively in the market for a home just like your listing.

#### Updates and insights

Leverage Matrix's built-in functions to follow price changes, market conditions, or status updates. Or provide your clients with an in-depth analysis of supply and demand dynamics and how they drive the market's average days on market and trends (is the market going up, down, or is it stable?) with MarketStats by ShowingTime+. Distribute these reports to your clients and position yourself as the expert with the freshest data and sharpest insight in the market.

#### Collaborate and simplify

Your clients utilize[OneHome](https://corporate.onekeymls.com/products/onehome) [**™**](https://corporate.onekeymls.com/products/onehome)to track listings, request showings, and communicate with you about their preferences, from the moment they start looking for a home to the time you close their transaction and you can stay on top of all activity. Share a workspace with your clients, then interact with them in real time about listings and property searches, enhancing transparency and efficiency. Set up custom alerts and communications for them, so they're always in the know about new listings that meet their criteria, and can provide instant feedback to you.
