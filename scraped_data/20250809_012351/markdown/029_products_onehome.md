### Stay in the center of every transaction with OneHome™, a next-generation client portal, available to Matrix™ users, ready to revolutionize your clients’ home buying experience

[OneHome™ Support](https://support.onekeymls.com/hc/en-us/articles/29453420437780-OneHome-Resources)

## Tomorrow’s client collaboration portal, today.

#### Intuitive and insightful

OneHome™ promotes agent/client collaboration by learning about what clients want — and don’t want — in their next home. A short questionnaire identifies clients’ top preferences and features to establish their PropertyFit™, a special score that helps agents and clients assess the degree to which the listing meets their needs. Planner helps them learn about and track the buying or selling process.

#### Focused and interactive

Stay central to the transaction by providing your clients with your branded portal. OneHome™ enables them to request a tour, estimate monthly costs, provide feedback, or favorite a property. Send customized search results to a client so they see listings you know are right for them. Enable them to compare homes in the portal, or pull market insights about a neighborhood. Attach your notes and insights to listings you suggest to clients and highlight your market expertise.

#### Rich school data

OneHome™ provides critical school ratings information that compares the scores of elementary, middle, high, and private schools to schools across the OneKey® MLS service area.

#### Synchronized with Matrix™

Client activity and feedback from OneHome™ is synchronized with Matrix™, so there’s only one place to go to manage the OneHome™ portal. You can see when clients logged into the portal, opened your emails, saved searches on OneHome, favorited or discarded listings, or created notes.
