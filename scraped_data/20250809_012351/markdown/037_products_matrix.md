### OneKey® MLS utilizes Matrix™ to make hundreds of thousands of listings instantly accessible to every professional in our MLS. From brilliantly easy listing input to outstanding client collaboration tools, Matrix makes it simple to manage your transaction from origination to close.

[Matrix™ Support](https://support.onekeymls.com/hc/en-us/articles/29458360577044-Matrix-Resources)

## Drive client happiness.

#### Manage your listings and clients in Matrix™

Use the power of Matrix™ to find, manage, and research listings in OneKey® MLS. Built for professionals who demand superior listing input tools, Matrix™ makes it simple to create, maintain, and market listings that attract attention and buyers. Deep integration with public records and tax data via its companion application, Realist®, enable you to research the history of any listing, compare properties, and focus on your business.

#### Get out of the office

Get into the field with MLS-Touch®, the application that puts Matrix™ in the palm of your hand. Collaborate with your clients, edit listings, conduct research, or put together a quick CMA. Whatever you need to do, you can get it done wherever you happen to be.

#### Gauge the market's interest

Understand how your listing is performing with the Matrix™ Hit Counter. Every visit and click to your listing by an agent or consumer within Matrix™ Or OneHome™ is tracked, so you know which of your listings are favorites or discards. Share listing performance easily with clients and offer factual support for decisions like price changes.

#### Stay ahead of the market

Follow the areas you work in and the property types you sell in real time with Matrix™ Hot Sheets. Know which properties had a price change, sold, went off-market, or cameon the market first, and help your clients get ahead of the market. Be the expert.

#### Boost listing velocity

Leverage Matrix™ Reverse Prospecting and OneKey® MLS’s 43,000+ community of professionals to shorten the sales cycle for your listing. Query Matrix™ to find out which agents have a client with a saved search that is looking for a home just like your listing within the MLS. Then easily contact the agent with additional information or updates or to see if there's interest in your listing.
