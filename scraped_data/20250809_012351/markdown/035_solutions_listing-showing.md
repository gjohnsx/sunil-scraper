# Listing & Showing

### Listing and showing your property is a breeze in OneKey® MLS. From straightforward listing entry to streamlined showing services designed to take the guesswork out of scheduling, getting your property to market and close is as easy as 1-2-3

[Explore Solution Support](https://support.onekeymls.com/hc/en-us/categories/26660238626196-Listing-Showing)

### List, market, show, and close your listing with with integrated tools.

#### Simple Add/Edit

OneKey® MLS is built on the CoreLogic® Matrix™ platform, which features a can’t-miss, step-by-step add/edit module that enables you to upload a new listing with high resolution photos and virtual tours in a matter of minutes. Draw extra attention and ongoing engagement to your listing with a floor plan from CubiCasa. You can also update a listing from anywhere using MLS-Touch®, or within Matrix™ itself on any device. Pull in comprehensive tax data about your listing from Realist®, all at the touch of a button.

#### Schedule showings

Take the headache of scheduling showings away with ShowingTime™ for the MLS by setting your preferences as the listing agent when you upload your listing. If you’re a buyer’s agent, you can create a cart for a client to handle multiple showings and even put the addresses in a logical order with drive times. Or use MLS-Touch® to manage showing requests from anywhere. Let clients in via SentriLock's Near Field Technology, even if your cellular network is down.

#### Screen tenants

Use RentSpree to automatically screen prospective tenants right within your listing. RentSpree is automatically included on thousands of rental listings on OneKeyMLS.com — presenting you with automatic opportunities to close more rentals every time your listing goes to market.

#### Help with down payments

Broaden exposure for your listing by letting your clients know about their eligibility for down payment assistance through Down Payment Resource. Every eligible listing on OneKeyMLS.com automatically bears the DPR icon so it’s instantly recognizable, and 79 percent of homes qualify.  Down payment assistance can be used for single family homes, multi-family, and condominiums, and programs offer up to $200,000 of assistance.
