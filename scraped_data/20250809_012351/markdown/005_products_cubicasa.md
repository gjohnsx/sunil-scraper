### CubiCasa delivers what consumers want most--second only to listing photos and data-- when home shopping online, an accurate floor plan. With CubiCasa, buyers can better understand the home’s square footage, layout, and flow to know if it will suit their needs

[CubiCasa Support](https://support.onekeymls.com/hc/en-us/articles/27215908506516-CubiCasa-Resources)

## Capture more leads.

#### Drive traffic and engagement

Eight in 10 consumers say they are more likely to view a home if it has a floor plan. According to the National Association of REALTORS®, consumers rank floor plans and virtual tours as “very useful” features and spend more time online with listings that include them.

#### Make listings memorable

While photos illustrate the look of a listing, floor plans inform the spatial “feel” of a place in a way an image never can. Floor plans enable consumers to move from room to room so they can grasp the entire space from a bird's eye view, and make every listing memorable.

#### Get more qualified buyers

Buyers who look at floor plans know if a listing will work for them — so they don’t waste your time on listings that aren’t right. Floor plans help buyers qualify themselves.

#### Scan to create your floor plan in five minutes

Use your mobile device to scan the listing to generate a professional, sophisticated floor plan that includes room dimensions, fixed furniture, and total square footage. Style it and include your logo. Download the results in 6-24 hours, and insert into your MLS listing.

#### Create your account to get started

Every real estate professional can use CubiCasa LITE as part of their OneKey® MLS subscription. To create your account, visit the[CubiCasa website](https://www.cubi.casa/), and download the mobile app to get started. You can also upgrade your CubiCasa plan at a special OneKey® MLS-only rate to take advantage of even more functionality in the mobile app. You can also save 10% when you reach 20 or more paid scans per month, and all paid add-ons count towards the volume discount.
